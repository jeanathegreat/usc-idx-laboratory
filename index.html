Vision

CEAC sees itself as a self-sustained laboratory equipped with up-to-date IT facilities and managed by competent staff. It is a modern laboratory equipped with computer hardware and software for academic learning and trainings.

Mission

We aim to automate office works to reduce the need of human work and to make use of time efficiently to satisfy the needs of the students and faculty members. It is equipped with the latest IT facilities to remain responsive to the needs of College of Engineering especially that of the B.S. Computer Engineering curriculum. It is our commitment to expose our students with the latest hardware and software facilities to develop highly skilled and competent graduates.

We aim to provide efficient service by recruiting, training, and retaining competent and responsible staff. The core values for CEAC Staff includes honesty, trustworthiness, client-centeredness, commitment to work, leadership, orderliness, and relationship.

CEAC further aims to be a venue for university-wide, local, national, and international IT events.
